HISTORY OF VIDEO GAMES CONSOLES

In collaboration with MAXXI (National Museum of 21st Century Arts), we invite you to visit the genesis of video games consoles. From the progenitor Magnafox Odyssey to the successor Steam Deck, the exhibition will take you on a journey through the history of video games consoles.

EXPLORE THE EXHIBITION
WHAT IS HVAC?

History of Video Games Consoles (HVAC) are an exhibition that dives deep into the history of video games consoles. Its inception towards the human history becomes the genesis for a behemoth form of entertainment with thriving industry and communities.
Behind these video games consoles also lies hopes and dreams of many people that begin their dream of finding a new way of having fun. Instead of reinventing the wheel, these video games consoles simply opened a portal to a new universe, leading to a golden era of interactive media entertainment.
